# AI Image Prompt Templates

## Midjourney Prompt Formula
```
[Subject + Context], [Style], [Lighting], [Color palette], [Composition], [Mood], --ar [ratio] --v 6 --q 2
```

## DALL-E 3 Prompt Formula
```
[Style descriptor], [Subject description], [Color scheme], [Composition notes], [What to avoid], [Format]
```

## Templates Theo Ngành

### Y Tế / Sức Khỏe — Phong cách ấm áp, không lạnh lẽo

**Infographic / Educational:**
```midjourney
Flat design medical infographic, [topic: pregnant woman / baby development / health tips],
soft pastel colors [mint green #E8F5F0, navy #1B3A4B, blush pink #FFD6E0],
clean minimal layout, educational poster style, white background,
professional yet warm, no text (placeholder only), --ar 1:1 --v 6
```

```dall-e
Minimalist flat design illustration for healthcare infographic,
[subject description], warm pastel color palette with mint and navy,
clean composition with space for text overlay, professional medical aesthetic,
avoid: clinical cold feeling, stock photo look, dark colors
```

**Bác sĩ / Personal brand:**
```midjourney
Professional portrait photography style, [Asian] doctor in white coat,
warm studio lighting, soft bokeh background [clinic / nature / neutral],
approachable and trustworthy expression, warm color grading,
NOT harsh clinical lighting, --ar 4:5 --v 6
```

**Background / Texture cho social post:**
```midjourney
Soft abstract background, [color: mint green / blush pink / warm white],
subtle texture, minimal, suitable for text overlay, healthcare aesthetic,
clean and professional, --ar 1:1 --v 6
```

## Canva AI Text-to-Image Prompts

Canva AI hoạt động tốt với prompts ngắn, mô tả rõ style:

```
[Style: Flat design / Watercolor / 3D render / Photograph]
[Subject: ...]  
[Colors: ...]
[Mood: warm / professional / playful / minimal]
```

**Ví dụ cho y tế:**
```
Flat design illustration, pregnant woman meditation, soft mint and pink colors, 
warm and calming mood, minimal style, white background
```

---

## Negative Prompts (Dùng với Midjourney --no)

**Tránh cho ngành y tế:**
```
--no surgery, blood, hospital equipment, cold clinical environment, 
stock photo, fake smile, harsh lighting, dark mood, Western features (nếu target VN)
```

**Tránh chung:**
```
--no blur, watermark, text in image, low quality, distorted hands, 
extra limbs, poor composition, oversaturated
```

## Lưu ý
- **Khuôn mặt thật:** Midjourney thường tạo khuôn mặt không thực tế — không dùng để đại diện cho bác sĩ thật. Dùng ảnh thật của bác sĩ, chỉ dùng AI cho illustration/background/element.
- **Brand consistency:** Luôn dùng cùng color palette trong mọi prompt → visual identity nhất quán
- **Canva AI vs Midjourney:** Canva AI tốt cho graphic/flat design; Midjourney tốt hơn cho realistic photography style
- **Copyright:** AI-generated images có thể dùng cho thương mại tùy theo platform — check ToS của Midjourney/DALL-E trước khi dùng
