## 💡 7 Key Insights

### Insight 1: Luật Dân Số Content Is Wedge to Reposition Market (H2 Critical Path)
**Finding:** Luật Dân số bài (W20 bài #1) is strategically more important than improving general content quality.

**Evidence:**
- Market: First-mover advantage (no competitor has education content about this policy yet)
- Data: Expected 3x shares vs baseline medical content (H2)
- Timeline: Policy effective 1/7 — window closes after W22

**Implication:** Even if this bài doesn't meet normal KPI targets, its strategic value is in repositioning HAPPY as "trusted policy interpreter" not just "medical educator." Use this to reset market perception away from BS Cao scandal narrative.

**Action:** Monitor H2 weekly. If shares < 3x baseline by 24/5, increase distribution (ads, email, Zalo OA once setup).

---

### Insight 2: Reels Format Victory Will Cascade (H1 → Strategy Pivot)
**Finding:** If H1 confirms Reels 2x+ reach vs static, entire M2-M3 production shifts.

**Evidence:**
- Market: Facebook algorithm May 2026 = Reels-first (50% platform time, 22% higher engagement)
- Current state: HAPPY has 0 Reels (zero content in winning format)
- Plan: H1 measurement 26/5 → decision point week 3

**Implication:** This is a binary decision. If confirmed:
- Visual content budget: 70% video, 30% infographic (vs current 50/50)
- Design work: Canva shifts from static design to video editing templates
- BS Thư schedule: Increases filming frequency (possibly 2 days/week vs current 1-2 days/week scattered)

**Action:** Have contingency plan ready for H1 decision. If YES → brief Canva on video template system (May 20). If NO → investigate quality issue (probably script/tone, not format).

### Insight 3: Week 1 (W20) Is Not Full Ramp — It's Proof of Concept
**Finding:** Weekly direction W20 has only 4 bài, not the 5-6 average planned for M2-M3.

**Evidence:**

NOT expected: Hit KPI targets W20. W20 KPI miss is fine if systems + proof-of-concept confirmed.

**Action:** Manage expectations with BS Thư & team. W20 = learning week. Real scale starts W21.

### Insight 4: TikTok Strategy Lags Facebook By 1 Month (Intentional)
**Finding:** Facebook hero month 1, TikTok hero month 2-3 is NOT delay — it's strategic sequencing.

**Evidence:**
- Channel matrix: Facebook first (Reels, repurpose faster), TikTok second (needs algorithm trust built)
- Market: TikTok HCP (doctor) trust score takes 2-3 weeks watch time accumulation to manifest
- Resource: One BS Thư, one camera — can't ramp 2 channels simultaneously
- Personas: Thủy (urban) has TikTok habit; Hương (provincial) uses Facebook primarily

**Implication:** This is a feature, not a bug. Staggered ramp reduces quality risk:
1. W20-W21: Perfect Facebook Reels system (H1 measurement)
2. W22-W24: Add TikTok upload (reuse tested scripts + visuals)
3. W25-W30: Full TikTok optimization (H3 reply-video testing)

**Action:** Don't try to push TikTok in W20. Let Facebook establish proof-of-concept first. TikTok ramp is on schedule.

---

### Insight 5: Repurposing SOP Is Gating Factor for Speed (Not Creativity)
**Finding:** Repurposing brief (hero TikTok → Facebook Reels) is fully designed and SOP-ified.

**Evidence:**
- Repurposing brief: 5-step SOP for TikTok-to-Reels (SnapTik download → CapCut export → FB upload)
- Timing: Same-day upload for "freshness signal"
- Compliance: Watermark removal is non-negotiable
- Scale: 1 TikTok original → 1 Reels repost (every content piece, no exception)

**Implication:** W20-W30 output speed is bottlenecked on:
1. BS Thư filming schedule (bottleneck #1)
2. Copywriter generating script (bottleneck #2)
3. ~~Repurposing~~ (SOLVED — SOP exists, Claude can automate)

Production can scale to 10 bài/week IF copywrite + filming capacity increases. Repurposing is NOT limiting factor.

**Action:** Don't build more repurposing templates. Instead: (1) lock BS Thư on 2x/week filming minimum, (2) build copywriter capacity (Claude prompting vs hiring).

---

### Insight 6: Notion Database Is Single Source of Truth (But Schema Needs Discipline)
**Finding:** Content plan exists (56 pieces in calendar), but tracking won't work unless Notion database is updated religiously.

**Evidence:**
- Handoff brief: Execution Agent responsible for updating Notion status + actual results
- Hypothesis log: H1-H3 measurement depends on "Reach after 7 days" being filled in Notion
- A/B test log: Template exists but empty (awaiting W20 data)
- Content plan: 56-piece calendar is markdown file, not Notion (source of truth fragmented)

**Implication:** Success of hypothesis-driven optimization depends on discipline. If Notion not updated weekly:
- H1-H3 measurement fails → can't pivot strategy
- KPI tracking breaks → can't report to BS Thư
- Scalability planning fails → can't predict M2-M3 capacity

**Action:** Notion entry requirement = non-negotiable QA gate. Content Execution MUST fill "Reach", "ER%", "Status" within 24h of posting. Planner reviews every Friday.

---

### Insight 7: Compliance Framework Reduces Risk But Increases Content Friction
**Finding:** Handoff brief compliance rules are explicit and necessary, but they add review cycle.

**Evidence:**
- Compliance rules: No "chữa khỏi", "100%", no patient images without consent, no livestream exams
- Execution impact: Every script + visual must be reviewed before filming/publishing
- Current state: No formal compliance workflow exists (only noted in brief)
- Risk: Bài đăng rồi phát hiện vi phạm → damage control + repost

**Implication:** 
- SOP needed: Script → Planner compliance check → approve → BS Thư shoots
- Buffer: Add 24h compliance review in weekly timeline (affects W20 Luật Dân số posting if not done now)
- Training: Copywriter + designer need compliance guardrails in prompts (can't just say "exciting medical content")

**Action:** Create compliance checklist (5-7 items) that travels with every script/visual. Planner does final gate. Can't defer this to week 2.
